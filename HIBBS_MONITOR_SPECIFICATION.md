## 4. DATA PRESENTATION FORMAT FOR EACH INDICATOR

### 4.1 Economic Indicators - Data Display Patterns

**Format Type A: Large Metric + Chart**
- Large prominent number displayed (e.g., "4.0%", "4.4%", "+119,000")
- Accompanied by chart visualization below/adjacent
- Supporting text description in body
- Data source cited at bottom

**Examples on Page 2:**
- **Real GDP:** "4.0%" (annual rate, Q3 2025)
  - Chart: Line/area chart showing quarterly percentage changes
  - Supporting text: "The Atlanta FED estimates that the real gross domestic product (GDP) increased at an annual rate of 4.0% during the third quarter of 2025."
  - Notes: "the figures are seasonally adjusted. As of November 25, 2025."
  - Source: "The Federal Reserve Bank of Atlanta"

- **CPI-U:** "0.3%" (one-month percent change)
  - Chart: Line/area chart showing monthly percentage changes
  - Supporting text: "The Consumer Price Index for All Urban Consumers (CPI-U) rose by 0.3% in September 2025 following a 0.4% rise in August and 0.2% rise in July"
  - Notes: "the figures are seasonally adjusted."
  - Source: "U.S. Bureau of Labor Statistics"

### 4.2 Key Metrics Display Format

**Format Type B: KPI Card (Key Performance Indicator)**
- Large bold number in box/card format
- Label below number
- Supporting text if applicable
- Date qualifier in parentheses
- Source citation below

**Examples on Page 2:**
- **Unemployment Rate**
  - Number: 4.4%
  - Label: "UNEMPLOYMENT RATE"
  - Date: "(September 2025)"
  - Source: "Source: U.S Bureau of Labor Statistics"

- **Jobs**
  - Number: +119,000
  - Label: "JOBS"
  - Date: "(September 2025)"
  - Notes: "the figures are seasonally adjusted."
  - Source: "Source: U.S Bureau of Labor Statistics"

- **Federal Funds Rate**
  - Range: 3.75 - 4.0%
  - Label: "Federal Funds Rate"
  - Note: "FOMC statement as of October 29, 2025"
  - Source: "Source: U.S Bureau of Labor Statistics"

### 4.3 Regional/Texas Data Display Format

**Format Type C: Multi-column Metrics**

**Tax Data Section (Page 3):**
- Layout: 3-column arrangement
  - Column 1: Categories with percentages
    - "Utility Taxes" → "2.6%"
    - "Natural Gas Production" → "3.5%"
  - Column 2: "Total Tax Collections" with date qualifier "(October 2025)"
    - Large number: "$6.4B"
    - Supporting text describing trend
  - Column 3: "Share of Tax Collection by Fund in Texas Sales Tax" with chart

- Total Sales Tax Revenue: "$6.2M" (Tyler MSA specific)

**Employment Growth Section (Page 3):**
- Format: Split indicators
  - "TEXAS" column
    - Number: "+179,898"
    - Label: "JOBS YoY"
    - Date: "(August 2025)"
  - "TYLER MSA" column
    - Number: "+2,710"
    - Label: "JOBS YoY"
    - Date: "(August 2025)"
  - Supporting text: "In August 2025, Texas employment rose by 3.2% and Tyler MSA employment decreased by 2.1%"

**Mortgage Rates Section (Page 3):**
- Format: 2-column side-by-side
  - Column 1: "30-Yr FRM" → "6.26%"
  - Column 2: "15-Yr FRM" → "5.54%"
  - Label: "Mortgage Rates"
  - Note: "U.S. weekly averages as of 11/20/2025"
  - Source: "Freddie Mac"

### 4.4 Data Precision & Number Formatting

- **Percentages:** 
  - GDP/CPI: One decimal place (e.g., 4.0%, 0.3%)
  - Mortgage rates: Two decimal places (e.g., 6.26%, 5.54%)
  - Employment growth: One decimal place (e.g., 3.2%, -2.1%)
  - Tax categories: One decimal place (e.g., 2.6%, 3.5%)

- **Large numbers:**
  - With "+" prefix for positive employment changes: "+179,898", "+2,710"
  - With "$" prefix for monetary amounts: "$6.4B", "$6.2M"
  - Commas for thousands separator

- **Currency formatting:**
  - Billions abbreviated as "B": $6.4B
  - Millions abbreviated as "M": $6.2M

## 7. SPECIFIC FORMATTING RULES

### 7.1 Font Specifications

**Primary Fonts Used:**
1. **OpenSans** (sans-serif) - Headlines and data labels
   - Weights: Light, Bold, SemiBold
   - Sizes: 21.3pt, 25.8pt, 68.6pt (titles); 12.1-13.3pt (section headers)

2. **Rubik** (sans-serif) - Special emphasis
   - Weight: SemiBold
   - Size: 68.6pt (primary title emphasis)

3. **Cambria** (serif) - Body text and descriptions
   - Weights: Regular, Bold, Italic
   - Sizes: 6pt (smallest notes), 7pt-8pt (source citations), 12pt-12.8pt (body text)

### 7.2 Color Scheme

**Colors Observed:**
- **Primary Text:** Black (#000000)
- **Chart Colors:** Multiple colors for different data series
  - Likely includes: Blues, oranges, grays for distinction
- **Backgrounds:** White (#FFFFFF)
- **Accent Colors:** Not heavily used; minimal colored highlights

### 7.3 Highlighting & Emphasis

**Large Numbers:**
- Placed in prominent positions
- Larger font sizes (12-15pt+ for key metrics)
- Bold weight for emphasis
- Sometimes in boxed/highlighted format

**Data Sources:**
- Small italic text (6-8pt)
- Positioned below each data section
- Consistent format: "Source: [Agency/Institution]"

**Notes/Disclaimers:**
- Small italic text (7pt)
- Positioned below charts or indicators
- Format: "Notes: [explanation]"
- Sometimes includes date qualifiers

### 7.4 Borders & Boxes

- Minimal use of borders
- Some indicators may have light box/card styling
- Clean, minimal design approach

### 7.5 Spacing & Alignment

**Vertical Spacing:**
- ~15-20 points between major sections
- ~10 points between subsections
- ~5-10 points between related items
- Adequate white space maintained throughout

**Horizontal Alignment:**
- Centered: Title page content, some section headers
- Left-aligned: Body text, descriptions
- Distributed: Multi-column data layouts

## 9. DATA DISPLAY FORMAT ANALYSIS

### **Table vs. Text vs. Charts**

**Format Distribution:**
- **Charts:** Heavy use (3-4 per page on Pages 2-3)
  - Line/Area charts for time-series data (GDP, CPI-U)
  - Bar charts for comparative data
  - Pie/Donut charts for share breakdowns

- **Text:** Moderate use
  - Descriptive paragraphs for context
  - Supporting information for metrics
  - ~50-150 words per section

- **KPI Cards:** Regular use
  - Standalone metric boxes
  - Large numbers + small labels

- **Tables:** Minimal/None observed
  - Data primarily presented as cards or embedded in descriptive text

### **Numeric Precision Standards**

| Data Type | Precision | Examples |
|-----------|-----------|----------|
| Percentages (rates) | 1 decimal | 4.0%, 0.3%, 3.2% |
| Mortgage rates | 2 decimals | 6.26%, 5.54% |
| Job counts | Whole numbers with commas | +179,898, +2,710 |
| Dollar amounts | Abbreviated with 1 decimal | $6.4B, $6.2M |
| Ranges | Both ends specified | 3.75 - 4.0% |
